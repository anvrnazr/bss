Admission Procedure

Admission to all classes is open every year by the month of April. a student who wishes to be a Gurukulam product should ensure it by filling up the application form supplied in the office. To check their previous knowledge an Entrance Exam is conducted for all classes. Selected students name list will be furnished the next day of the exam as per the rank list. the very next day these students along with their parents will be called on for a personal interview with the Principal. we provide a counselling session where we publish the fees structure with transparency. It also open up a room to show case all the schools activities with a video presentation. we on who ready to accept the norms furnished earlier becomes a member of Gurukulam family - The Different School.
